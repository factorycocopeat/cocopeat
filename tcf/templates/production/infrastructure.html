Infrastructure

Fibre Family has its head office situated at Alappuzha, Kerala. Our manufacturing unit is established at the Coconut City of Pollachi, Tamil Nadu. We start by processing the raw coconut husks collected from graded coconut plantations around the area, to extract coir fibre and the spongy material that binds the coconut fibre in the husk, the coir / coco pith (peat), the main constituent for Coco / Coir Growing Medium.

Our Facilities Includes

  • Coconut Husk Decortication Unit
  • 80,000 Sq.Ft of paved Coco Peat Drying Yard
  • Poly-houses for drying Coco Peat during off-season
  • State-of-the-art Coco Peat Screening Unit including multi-stage Graders and Destoner
  • 10 Hydraulic Presses manufacturing a variety of Coco Peat and Coir Garden Articles Products
  • Fully Automated Weigh Metric Production facility
  • Fully Automated Packing Units
  • Dozers, Tractors, Fork Lifts and Peat Turners
  • Logistics fleet comprising of 5 Trucks and 3 Prime Movers
  • In-house 60T weigh-bridge
  • 100% solar powered office

Professional grower’s one stop shop – The CocoPeat Factory Substrates

We manufacture coco peat and coco chips based substrates for the professional growers across the globe. Not just manufacturing, we actually grow on them. We test our substrates in our own green house to make sure that they are perfect for the crops to root in and flourish.
