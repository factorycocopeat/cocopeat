We, The CocoPeat Factory!

We are the leading manufacturer, exporter and wholesaler of premium Coir Products from India. Our product range includes Coco Peat substrates, Erosion Control coir products and Coir Garden Articles. Our parent company, Techno Exports, is a multi-time National Award winning, ISO certified, One Star Export House. The CocoPeat Factory was incepted in the year 2003 as a sister concern. We manufacture and export high quality coco peat substrates, coir garden articles and erosion control products.

Coir & Coco Peat Manufacturer

Our manufacturing process starts by processing raw coconut husks. Unlike many competitors, we rely mainly on our own production, giving us greater control over the quality of our raw materials. We procure husks from nearby plantations, which are then processed into Coconut Fibre and Coco Peat. The fibre is used to manufacture erosion control products and garden articles, while the raw Coco Peat is washed, sun-dried in concreted dry-yards, and screened to remove stones and sand. Finally, the coco peat is compacted to produce a wide range of substrates.
